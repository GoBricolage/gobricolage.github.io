Cabinets

The Bricolage Cabinet division designs and manufactures cabinets, shelving, countertops and carts, no matter how many you need, and we do it fast. We specialize in melamine, stainless steel, plastics and 80/20 components. Various combinations of materials can be used to achieve your desired result.

We offer off-the-shelf hardware and locks. If stock products don’t meet your requirements, we will design and build whatever you like.

Our caster inventory includes several styles to meet a variety of needs. If our selection does not meet your needs, we will find the appropriate product for your project.

For more information on Cabinets, or if you would like a quote, email us or call 614-853-6789.
