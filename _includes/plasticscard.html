Plastics

The Bricolage Plastics division has the engineering, design and manufacturing capability to quickly convert your ideas into high-quality products that meet your needs. Our basic capabilities combined with proprietary, computerized design and manufacturing resources let us deliver specialized products to you in a short amount of time. We can ship a prototype if desired. We design products using 3D and articulation software.

When you approve your design the CAD specifications go to our wide range of CNC equipment, including waterjet cutting, to quickly manufacture and ship your order or prototype. We stock many types of plastic for immediate use to fulfill your special requirements. Our inventory includes a wide range of sizes, thicknesses and colors of acrylic, polycarbonate, PETG, PVC, polystyrene, HDPE, polyethylene, various foams and other types of plastic. If necessary, we can also order non-stock plastic for your project.

Our dedicated team of engineers, designers, fabricators, welders and machinists will quickly respond to your needs. Services include hand fabrication, heat and press bending; welding includes solvent and adhesive, mechanical fasteners, thermoforming and molds, articulating components and locking systems and machining.

Additional plastic fabrication services include laser engraving and etching, CNC engraving, heat stamping and many types of adhesive label systems and signage substrates.

For more information on Plastics, or if you would like a quote, email us or call 614-853-6789.
